REGISTRATION / FIRST TEMPORARY RESIDENCE PERMIT  
 DOCUMENT  
   
AFTER  THE ARRIVAL OF THE THIRD COUNTRY NATIONAL  
1 Copy of valid passport or other travel document with validity of at least three  (3) months  from the date of the 
submission of the application.  
2 Copy of passport or other travel document page that shows the last entry in the Republic.  
3 For employees  working  for a company registered abroad :  
o Employment  contract  of indefinite  duration  or of at least  equal duration  to the requested residence permit,  
o employer ’s confirmation  that the applicant  can perform  his/her duties  remotely  and that the project  / work  
does not concern activity or provision of services to an employer registered in Cyprus,  
o duly stamped  copy  of the company’s registration certificate  abroad , stating  the legal name, registered base , 
activity field and corporate purpose of the company , 
o contact information of the company abroad,   
o information on the status of the applicant in the company.  
OR 
For self-employment:  
o Contract (s) of project or  of services with a duration of at least equal to the requested residence permit.  
o Confirmation  by the applicant  that (s)he can perform his duties remotely and that the project does not concern 
activity or provision of services to a client established  in Cyprus.  
 
4 Evidence that the applicant has sufficient and stable monthly living resources amounting to at least €3500 , after  
the deduction of contributions and taxes, from:  
o proof of salary payment showing the net income *, and  
o bank account (detailed bank statements of the last 6 months that prove the existence of a stable and 
satisfactory income outside the Republic).  
 
Note:  The above amount is increased by 20% for the spouse or partner  and by 15% for each child.  
 
* In cases where there is no proof of net income, a n Affirmation  is required according to which the applicant will 
certify that his /her  net income, after the deduction of contributions and taxes  on the income  presented in the 
submitted documents, is not less than the minimum amount required on the basis of the defined income criteria.  
5 Original Certificate of Criminal Record from the country of origin (if the applicant resides in a country other than 
the country of origin, the certificate should be issued from the country of residence) . 
6 Original blood analysis results showing that the applicant  does not bear/suffer from HIV, syphilis, hepatitis B and 
C and chest x -rays for tuberculosis (TB), duly certified by a specialized Doctor.   
7 Certificate of Health Insurance for medical care that covers inpatient and outpatient care and transportation of 
corpse (Plan A).  
8 Title deed or rental agreement of a house/apartment, duly stamped if valued over €5000  AND corresponding 
utility bill.  
9 Affirmation  to which the applicant will commit  that (s)he or his /her  family members will not provide work or 
services or  project in any way to an employer or clients  established in Cyprus.
